Imran Khan

About Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a Pakistani politician and former cricketer. Born on October 5, 1952, in Lahore, he captained the Pakistan cricket team to its first-ever World Cup victory in 1992.

After retiring from cricket, he entered politics in 1996, founding the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party. Imran Khan served as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan from August 2018 to April 2022.

His leadership have made him highly popular in Pakistan.
